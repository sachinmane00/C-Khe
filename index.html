PRIVACY POLICY
Your privacy is fully protected. C-Khe does not collect any personal information. All learning data (XP, streaks, bookmarks) stays only on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.
1

Overview

C-Khe ("the App") is a learning app developed for students. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App does and does not collect.

Last updated: February 25, 2026

2

Data We Do NOT Collect

C-Khe does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. The App does not require account creation, login, or registration of any kind.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personal data — ever.

3

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device using Android's local storage. This data never leaves your device:

  • Your XP points and level
  • Your daily study streak count
  • Bookmarked cards
  • Recently searched topics
  • Onboarding selections (board and class)

This data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the App.

5

Children's Privacy

C-Khe is designed for students, including those under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users. The App is fully functional without any personal data.

6

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics platforms, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. No data is shared with or sold to any third party.

7

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
